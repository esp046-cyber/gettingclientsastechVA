üìÖThe Weekly Planning System

Sunday Planning Ritual (30 minutes):

1 Review Last Week

Check time tracking data. What went well? What took longer than expected? Adjust estimates.

2 List All Commitments

Client deliverables, meetings, deadlines. Get everything out of your head and onto paper/screen.

3 Prioritize Using Eisenhower Matrix
  • Urgent + Important: Client deadlines, urgent fixes (do first)
  • Important, Not Urgent: Portfolio work, learning, business dev (schedule time)
  • Urgent, Not Important: Some emails, calls (batch/delegate if possible)
  • Neither: Social media scrolling, busywork (eliminate)
4 Time Block Your Week

Assign specific time slots for each major task. Example: Monday 9-12 = Client A automation, 1-3 = Client B content, 3-4 = Proposals

5 Build in Buffer Time

Add 20% buffer for unexpected issues, calls, revisions. Don't schedule every minute!
